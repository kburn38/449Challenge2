Introducing Color-Mix!

Using this function, you can mix two colors into whatever color you like!

Blue + Green
Red + Blue
#fcba03 + #03fc28
#fc03e8 + #fca503

You can even choose from an array of color spaces!

Blue + Green in Linear-sRGB
Blue + Green in HSL
Blue + Green in HWB
Blue + Green in LCH
Blue + Green in OKLCH
Blue + Green in LAB
Blue + Green in OKLAB
Blue + Green in XYZ

Want to try it? Use this in your CSS: color-mix(in [Desired Color Space], [First Color Value], [Second Color Value] [Transparency])
